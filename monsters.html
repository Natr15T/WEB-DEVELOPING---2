Famous Monsters

Rathalos

TypeFlying Wyvern
ElementFire
WeaknessDragon, Thunder
HabitatAncient Forest
Threat LevelHigh

Rathalos are male Flying Wyverns introduced in Monster Hunter. Having appeared in every single Monster Hunter game in the series, including spin-offs, Rathalos are considered an icon of the Monster Hunter series.

Rathalos are large, bipedal wyverns with a spiny, armored hide. It is primarily bright red with black markings. They possess a flame sac used to produce deadly flaming projectiles, and poisonous talons that inflict toxic wounds. Their thick tail ends in a spiked club.

Expert flyers, Rathalos hunt from the skies, burning prey with fireballs or slashing with poisonous talons. On land, they use their legs, tail, and teeth for devastating attacks.

Diablos

TypeFlying Wyvern
ElementNone
WeaknessIce, Dragon
HabitatWildspire Waste
Threat LevelVery High

Diablos are large, bipedal wyverns with powerful armor. Known for their massive bull-like horns, tusk-like fangs, and a spiny neck frill. They have a studded tail used for defense.

They can burrow underground to ambush or escape, and produce a stunning roar. Diablos can charge with incredible speed, using their horns to maximize impact.

Though herbivores that feed on cacti, they are extremely territorial and aggressive, even fighting other Diablos for dominance.

Nergigante

TypeElder Dragon
ElementNone
WeaknessThunder, Dragon
HabitatRotten Vale, Elder's Recess
Threat LevelExtreme

Nergigante is a bristly Elder Dragon covered in regenerating spikes. With large horns and a black-and-purple hide, it grows white spikes that harden and increase its damage.

As these spikes mature, they turn black and can repel attacks. Hunters must break them before they harden to reduce its threat level.

Highly aggressive, Nergigante will attack anything on sight. It feeds on other Elder Dragons and is theorized to help regulate their population by hunting them.
